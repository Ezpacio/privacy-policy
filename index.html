Privacy Policy

Last Update: 17.01.2025

This privacy policy outlines our commitment to protecting the personal information of all users of the Women Workouts app. Please read this policy carefully.

Collected Data

Women Workouts does not collect any personal information from users. All data within the app (e.g., exercise count, calories burned, exercise days) is stored solely on the user's device and is not shared with third parties.

Data Storage and Security

The app stores all data locally in a database on your device. This information is visible only to the user and is not transferred to any server.

Third-Party Services

Women Workouts does not use third-party services or analytics tools. Therefore, no user data is shared with any other platform.

Content Usage

The exercise guides in the app are visually designed to help users perform the movements correctly. These guides are licensed and paid-for GIFs, and are used solely for informational and instructional purposes.

User Rights

Since no personal data is collected, there is no need to request access, correction, deletion, or portability of data. If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to contact us.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be announced within the app and/or on relevant platforms.
